# Project Retrospective

- Scope and WBS kept the project focused and on schedule
- Effective use of GitHub for version control and project management
- Clear documentation and organization of project files
- No major technical issues or hurdles encountered during development

**What could have gone better?**
- Would have liked to add more to project than what was initially planned in scope
- Grossly overestimated time needed for development and polish

- Start development earlier to allow for more time to experiment and iterate on design and content

Overall, I am satisfied with the final product and feel that it meets the project requirements while also serving as a strong portfolio piece. The project management techniques I used were effective in keeping me on track and ensuring that I met all deadlines. In the future, I will aim to push myself further and take more risks in my projects to create even more impressive results.
